City Latitude vs Max Temperature

As you can see from the scatter plot, the temperature rises the closer you get to the equator which is to be expected.

60 - 80 Latitude is closer to the North Pole (Alaska, Canada, Greenland etc) and the temperature has a significate drop.

What I would be interested in, is getting the (-60) - (-80) plots for the South Pole and see how dramatic it drops compared to the North Pole.
